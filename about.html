The Computer Club

Join the Computer Club as a first-time user or a more advanced user; receive support with all PC, iPad, Tablet and Smart Phone challenges; build networks and friendships. Mondays from 9:30am to 12:30pm and Tuesdays 1:00pm to 3:30pm during the School Term. The Computer Club is part of the Community Development Program which provides opportunities for the community to access creative, educational, social, health and wellbeing, as well as family friendly programs and activities. The Program also encourages and supports community led initiatives. The Community Development Program is funded by: Queensland Government Department of Communities, Child Safety and Disability Services
